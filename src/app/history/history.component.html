History

In this area, the historical sensor values over time are displayed. The data of the last 7 days are evaluated.

Fill Level

The fill level data are displayed on an hourly basis, only the last hours are displayed on a minutely basis. To aggregate the data, the mean value is calculated over the interval.

 

Coffee Consumption

Coffee consumption is calculated on a daily basis and displayed as a bar chart. The number indicates the number of coffee pots used.
